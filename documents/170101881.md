Terms and Definitions
=====================

A *Smart4Aviation Group* is understood as Smart4Aviation Technologies B.V., Smart4Aviation Americas, Inc., AzimuthIT Sp. z o. o. sp. k., AzimuthIT Sp. z o. o. or any other affiliate or subsidiary under common Smart4Aviation brand.

*Customer* is understood as airline or any other entity that Smart4Aviation Group is bound to with contractual obligations, including the entities that already entered the contract or are in talks about entering.

*GDPR* is understood as Regulation on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (Data Protection Directive).

*Personal data* is understood as any information relating to an identified or identifiable natural person; an identifiable natural person is one who can be identified, directly or indirectly, in particular by reference to an identifier such as a name, an identification number, location data, an online identifier or to one or more factors specific to the physical, physiological, genetic, mental, economic, cultural or social identity of that natural person (as defined in Regulation (EU) 2016/679 of the European Parliament and of the Council of 27 April 2016 on the protection of natural persons with regard to the processing of personal data and on the free movement of such data, and repealing Directive 95/46/EC (General Data Protection Regulation)).

Purpose
=======

This policy describes the approach to managing the security classification of information assets at Smart4Aviation Group, and outlines the standards by which information assets will be managed. Information is given the sensitivity level, and all personnel that would use the information should take necessary precautions when processing it, in accordance with the sensitivity level.

Objectives
==========

* Provide guidance for Smart4Aviation Group personnel about how to treat certain information in respect to security, privacy and confidentiality.
* To map data protection needs to security controls in terms of risks mitigation.
* To comply with industry standards, Smart4Aviation Group Customers' requirements regarding confidentiality and legal requirements (e.g. GDPR).

Scope
=====

All personnel of Smart4Aviation Group, including:

* employees of Smart4Aviation Group organizations,
* contractors or subcontractors of contractors of the Smart4Aviation Group organizations,
* any persons that conduct business tasks, directly or indirectly on behalf of any of Smart4Aviation Group organizations,

who process any information assets that are created, managed or otherwise obtained by Smart4Aviation Group.

Information Confidentiality Clause Mapping
==========================================

Given that:

* any Smart4Aviation Group information assets must be used with explicit Smart4Aviation Group permission, taylored to position's or contract's needs, and given on first day at work or contract service start, and
* Confidentiality Clauses were signed by all personnel involved in performing Smart4Aviation Group business, either as standalone document or as a part of a service contract,

the following mapping of Information Sensitivity Levels is introduced:

| **Information Sensitivity Level** | **Confidentiality Clauses Coverage** |
| --- | --- |
| **Secret** internal or Customer data | Those levels ARE COVERED by Confidentiality Clause that every  employeeor contractor of Smart4Aviation Group signed upon starting their work of services |
| **Private**internal or Customer data |
| **Restricted** internal data |
| **Sensitive** internal data |
| **Disclosable** internal data or public data | This level IS NOT COVERED by Smart4Aviation Group Confidentiality Clause. |

Reference Information Categories and Sensitivity Levels
=======================================================

The general rule is as follows:

* *Secret* internal or Customer data is intended to C-level Management or individual Managers or Specialists,
* *Private* internal or Customer data is intended for authorized staff on need-to-know basis. In order to achieve compliance with privacy laws in force, such as GDPR, they have separate detailed rules of processing described in Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpieczeństwa Danych Osobowych) and Rules of personal data protection (Regulamin ochrony danych osobowych).
* *Restricted* internal data is operated on by Management or Specialists,
* *Sensitive* internal data is allowed for all personnel,
* *Disclosable* data is allowed for all.

General provisions
==================

* When a person is revealed an information of Restricted, Private or Secret level that she/he is not tasked to use it for her duties, it should be considered a case of security incident and reported as Jira SECURITY ticket according to relevant [Security Incident Reporting Procedure](https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/display/S4A/Security+Incident+Reporting+Procedure).
* Responsibility for the proper handling of information according to its Information Sensitivity Level is on any person of Smart4Aviation Group. Failing to comply will be penalized according to the specifics of this person's contract with her/his organization within Smart4Aviation Group, in accordance with Criminal Law regulations.
* Any personal data shall be treated at least as Private level. Where business purpose of the data warrants classification as Secret level, explicit authorization and any rules and security controls as stipulated by GDPR,  Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpieczeństwa Danych Osobowych) and Rules of personal data protection (Regulamin ochrony danych osobowych) must apply.
