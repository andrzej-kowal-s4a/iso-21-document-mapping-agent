========

3. Remove employee from groups and aliases in LDAP (with VPN deactivation)
--------------------------------------------------------------------------

Go to <https://webldap.s4a.aero/ldapadmin/find_all.php> and type ID of removed employee and click first "invert selection" and then "Execute in phpLDAPadmin" to remove employee from groups, aliases and owned items.

There are also situations when currently removed subject is only recipient or owner which means it cannot be automatically erased from group/alias.

In that case employee manager or person on similar position should be contacted to find proper replacement or get confirmation about group/alias possibly removal.

20. Remove employee's device from Bitdefender console:
------------------------------------------------------

Go to <https://gravityzone.bitdefender.com>  
*Monitoring > Inventory > Physical devices*

Find employee's device by *NAME* or *LABEL,* both should be in format **jdoe** (first letter of name + last name. Column *Name* should have "-*brand*" after it, e.g. jdoe-lenovo).

Mark the chosen device and remove it from the console.
