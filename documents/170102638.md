Process flow
============

* Each unplanned downtime or service degradation should be treated as major incident
* Notify S4A Support Team using emergency line about the incident
* S4A Support Team notifies Customer representative accordingly to the set up communication channel. In parallel inform S4A Support Manager and S4A Key Account Manager.
* If required join the emergency bridge
* Start the bug triage process
* Perform fixing / recovery activities - as agreed with Customer
* Provide an status update to Customer with agreed periods
* Once the service is restored ask for Customer validation
* After Customer confirmation major incident can be closed - inform Customer
* Start the problem management activities, prepare RCA
* Inform Customer about RC and possible preventive actions
