Purpose
=======

The purpose of this policy is to outline the proper disposal/sanitization/destruction of media (physical or electronic). These rules are in place to protect sensitive and classified information, employees and Smart4Aviation company. Inappropriate disposal of media may put employees and Smart4Aviation company at risk.

Scope
=====

This policy applies to all employees, contractors, temporary staff, and other workers, with access to sensitive and classified data, and media. This policy applies to all equipment that processes, stores, and/or transmits classified and sensitive data.

Policy
======

When no longer usable, hard drives, diskettes, tape cartridges, CDs, ribbons, hard copies, print-outs, and other similar items used to process, store and/or transmit classified and sensitive data shall be properly disposed of in accordance with measures established by the following procedures:

* Clean Desk Policy / Polityka Czystego Biurka
* Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
* Sanitisation of devices, equipment or removable media before re-use or disposal [hardware] [utilisation]

The media that have been used to process, store, or transmit and/or sensitive and classified information shall not be released from Smart4Aviation‘s control until the equipment has been sanitized and all stored information has been cleared using one of the above methods.
