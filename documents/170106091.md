**Entry criteria:**

* Created CRB Page
* Impact assesment prior to the meeting:
  + products
  + projects
  + resources
  + finance - payments
  + suite
* Proposed scenarios for the decision
* CR scheduled on the agenda/ awaiting approval

**Exit Criteria:**

* Decision

1. **Procedure change presentation:**  
   1. **DRAFT:** Change requestor creates a Confluence page (Jira)? and provides possible scenarios.
   2. **IMPACT ASSESMENT**: CRB requestor ask for impact information on:  
      1. Products (prior to the meeting/ offline decision) :
      2. Resources (prior to the meeting/ offline decision):
      3. Projects (prior to the meeting/ offline decision) - impact to be discussed during PM call :
      4. Finances  (prior to the meeting/ offline decision)
      5. Company (if needed)
   3. **READY 4 CRB:** Once all data is provided, CRB requestor informs CRB moderator. CRB moderator adds information to the CRB page and informs requested attendants
   4. Change can be approved offline or will be reviewed during CRB meeting. If there will be no agreement, dedicated meeting to be scheduled.
2. **Procedure of change approval: (or )**  

   Approval means you accept impact defined in change request and execution is feasible.

   Approval from top:

   * If change is approved by CEO then change is consider as approved.
     + CIO, CFO and directors will execute this change.
   * If change is approved by CFO and CIO then change is consider as approved by CEO.
     + Directors will execute this change.
   * If change is approved by all directors reporting to CFO or CIO then change is consider as approved by CFO or CIO.
