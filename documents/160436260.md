This procedure describes process that should be executed during S4A customer off boarding

1. [Support+KAM] **Day Of Switch Off** (**DOSO**) minus **7 days**

   2
   2
   incomplete
   On-line meeting with Client to confirm that support services will end in one week discuss with Client operational team all the additional items that need to be performed (not listed below)
2. **DOSO**

   3
   3
   incomplete
   [Support] Customer is notified about service shutdown

   4
   4
   incomplete
   [Admins] VPN on S4A side is disabled (in general this is about Client connectivity to the environments) – please keep in mind that we want to have environment still running in case customer decides to switch on – Emergency Support Extension

   5
   5
   incomplete
   [Admins] Environment connectivity on S4A side is turned off – please keep in mind that we want to have environment still running in case customer decides to switch on – Emergency Support Extension

   6
   6
   incomplete
   [Admins] Restrict access to the Env (only administrative access allowed)

   7
   7
   incomplete
   [Optional] [Admins] redirect Env to dedicated "End of Support Page" to let know end users about the change
3. [Support] From now on every request send to support is replied to customer with information: Support Agreement is end of Live please contact on Your side the System Owner: XXXXX

   8
   8
   incomplete
    [Support] Setup auto-responder sd-JIRA - mail-adapter
4. [Admins] **DOSO +7 days** – bring the environment down and perform encrypted Backup of ENV (if required by customer)

   9
   9
   incomplete
   [Admins] Perform encrypted backup, if required by customer and we have no option e.g. to just shutdown servers
5. [Admins+Support] **DOSO + 21d** – Destroy environment (Point Of No Return), destroy Backup and all the customer data
