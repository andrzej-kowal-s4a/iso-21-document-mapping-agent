# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.10 Acceptable use of information and other associated assets

## 1. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy directly addresses the control requirements by establishing comprehensive rules for acceptable use of Smart4Aviation's information technology resources. It defines permitted and prohibited uses, expected behaviors, monitoring activities, and procedures for handling information across its full lifecycle including authentication, data protection, communication, internet use, and physical security requirements. The policy ensures personnel and external party users are made aware of information security requirements for protecting and handling information and other associated assets.

## 2. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is essential for the control as it establishes the information classification scheme (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) and defines specific handling rules for each classification level. It ensures that information and assets are protected according to their sensitivity level, which directly supports the acceptable use requirements in Control 5.10. The policy provides the foundation for drawing up acceptable use procedures for the full information life cycle in accordance with classification and determined risks.

## 3. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy addresses the disposal phase of the information lifecycle as required by Control 5.10. It outlines proper disposal/sanitization/destruction procedures for media containing sensitive information, ensuring authorized deletion methods are followed and information remains protected until final disposal. The policy ensures that information and other associated assets, when deleted or disposed, are handled in a secure manner as required by the control.

## 4. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure provides detailed steps for disposing of electronic hardware and ensures data is properly sanitized before disposal. It directly supports Control 5.10's requirement for authorization of disposal of information and assets and supported deletion methods, including committee oversight and proper documentation. The procedure ensures that storage media are properly marked and that disposal is authorized and documented.

## 5. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure establishes systematic monitoring of software installed on company devices to ensure compliance with acceptable use policies. It implements regular audits comparing installed software against approved/prohibited lists, directly supporting Control 5.10's requirement for monitoring activities and enforcement of acceptable use rules. The procedure ensures that maintenance of a record of authorized users of information and other associated assets is maintained.
