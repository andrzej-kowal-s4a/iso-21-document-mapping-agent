# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.20 Addressing information security within supplier agreements

## 1. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This is the most directly relevant document as it comprehensively covers supplier agreements and information security requirements. The policy establishes security requirements for supplier relationships including breach notification, compliance, confidentiality, data security, incident response, right to audit, and supply chain security. It details the process for establishing security requirements in contracts, RFI procedures, contract negotiations, and includes provisions for NDA and DPA agreements. The document aligns perfectly with ISO 27001 control 5.20 requirements for establishing and agreeing information security requirements with suppliers.

## 2. Company Managed Service Agreements
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108066

This document is highly relevant as it establishes the framework for Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and Operational Level Agreements (OLAs) with suppliers. It covers the negotiation process, service level targets, responsibilities of both parties, and monitoring mechanisms. The document addresses how service agreements formalize needs and expectations, which directly supports the ISO control requirement for establishing clear understanding between the organization and suppliers regarding information security obligations.

## 3. Client contract termination checklist - customer offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160436260

This procedure is relevant because ISO 27001 control 5.20 specifically requires termination clauses in supplier agreements including records management, return of assets, secure disposal of information, and ongoing confidentiality obligations. This document provides a comprehensive checklist for contract termination that includes access revocation, data retrieval, encrypted backups, secure data destruction, and proper asset return procedures, ensuring all termination requirements are met.

## 4. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure is relevant because control 5.20 requires incident management requirements and procedures in supplier agreements, especially notification and collaboration during incident remediation. This document establishes the incident reporting framework, notification procedures, and incident management processes that should be included in supplier agreements. It ensures suppliers understand their obligations for reporting security incidents and collaborating during incident response.

## 5. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This document is relevant because control 5.20 requires addressing legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements including data protection and handling of personally identifiable information (PII). The procedure establishes GDPR compliance requirements, breach notification procedures, and data protection measures that must be included in supplier agreements when suppliers handle personal data. It ensures suppliers understand their data protection obligations and breach notification requirements.

## 6. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy is relevant because control 5.20 requires the right to audit supplier processes and controls related to the agreement. This document establishes the organization's audit framework, audit program, and audit procedures. It demonstrates how the organization conducts audits and what expectations should be set with suppliers regarding audit access, audit procedures, and remediation of audit findings, which are essential elements to include in supplier agreements.
