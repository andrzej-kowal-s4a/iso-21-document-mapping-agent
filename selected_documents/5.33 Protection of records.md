# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.33 Protection of records

## 1. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy is directly relevant as it establishes guidelines for the proper disposal, sanitization, and destruction of media (both physical and electronic) used to process, store, and transmit classified and sensitive data. The policy ensures that records are protected from unauthorized access and release by requiring proper disposal procedures before media is released from Smart4Aviation's control. It addresses guidance point (a) by providing guidelines on storage, handling, chain of custody, and disposal of records, preventing manipulation and unauthorized access to records.

## 2. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure is highly relevant as it establishes specific disposal procedures for electronic equipment and includes retention requirements for disposal records. The procedure specifies that utilization protocols must be kept for 5 years from disposal, which demonstrates a retention schedule for disposal records (guidance point b). It also ensures proper chain of custody through committee oversight and documentation requirements, protecting records from loss and ensuring proper handling throughout the disposal process.

## 3. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is relevant because it categorizes records into different types (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) with specific handling rules for each category, addressing the control requirement to categorize records into record types with details of retention periods. The policy defines how different categories of records (accounting records, personnel records, legal records, etc.) should be handled and protected, ensuring records are protected according to their classification level. It supports protection from unauthorized access and release by establishing clear handling rules based on information sensitivity.

## 4. Regulamin ochrony danych osobowych_Rules of personal data protection_BILINGUAL
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102152

This document is highly relevant as it establishes comprehensive procedures for protecting personal data records, including storage, handling, and disposal requirements. The document specifies that paper documents containing personal data must be destroyed using shredders or specialized companies, and prohibits improper disposal methods. It addresses protection from loss, destruction, falsification, and unauthorized access by establishing secure handling procedures for personal data records, including requirements for secure storage, clean desk policies, and proper disposal methods.

## 5. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This policy is relevant because it establishes IT asset management requirements including maintaining inventory of hardware and software assets, which supports proper identification and tracking of records. The policy also includes access management controls that protect records from unauthorized access, requiring individual user IDs, authentication, and authorization based on job responsibilities. It ensures physical security of information processing and storage facilities, protecting records from loss, destruction, and unauthorized access through appropriate security barriers and access controls.

## 6. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy is relevant because it establishes the organization's commitment to compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including those related to records retention and protection. The policy ensures that Smart4Aviation operates within appropriate legal boundaries and monitors compliance with applicable laws in the Netherlands, Poland, and the European Union, which directly supports the control's purpose of ensuring compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to records protection. It provides the framework for understanding and meeting legal obligations regarding records management and retention periods.
