# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.1 Policies for information security

## 1. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This is the primary umbrella document that acts as the main information security policy approved by top management (CIO). It defines the organization's approach to managing information security, establishes security objectives, assigns responsibilities, and commits to continual improvement. The policy explicitly states it acts as an umbrella document for all other security policies and standards, making it directly aligned with ISO 27001 control 5.1 requirements for a high-level information security policy approved by management.

## 2. Policy Owner vs Author of procedures, standards, guidances and processes
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102632

This document establishes the framework for policy development, review, approval, and communication processes. It defines roles and responsibilities for Policy Owners and Authors, outlines procedures for policy creation including stakeholder input, management approval, review cycles, and communication to relevant personnel. The document ensures policies are reviewed at planned intervals and properly communicated, directly supporting ISO 27001 control 5.1 requirements for policy approval, review, and communication.

## 4. Information Classification Policy

## 5. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
