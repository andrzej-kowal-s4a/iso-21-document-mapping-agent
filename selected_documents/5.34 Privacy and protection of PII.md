## 2. Regulamin ochrony danych osobowych_Rules of personal data protection_BILINGUAL
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102152

This document establishes comprehensive procedures for the preservation of privacy and protection of PII, addressing the control requirement to develop and implement procedures for PII protection. The document provides detailed rules for secure use of IT equipment, password policies, handling of paper documents containing personal data, secure transport of devices containing PII, and procedures for reporting suspected breaches. It ensures that all personnel processing personal data follow appropriate procedures to protect PII from unauthorized access, disclosure, modification, or destruction.

## 3. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure is highly relevant as it establishes procedures for detecting, assessing, and responding to personal data breaches, ensuring protection of PII from loss, destruction, falsification, and unauthorized access. The procedure includes breach reporting mechanisms, risk assessment criteria, notification requirements to supervisory authorities and data subjects, and breach register maintenance. It ensures compliance with GDPR requirements for breach management and demonstrates appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect PII, including incident response capabilities.

## 4. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is relevant because it establishes that any personal data shall be treated at least as Private level sensitivity, with Secret classification where warranted, ensuring appropriate protection measures are applied based on data sensitivity. The policy explicitly references GDPR compliance and links to Personal Data Protection Policy and Rules of personal data protection, ensuring that PII is classified and handled according to its sensitivity level. It supports the control requirement for appropriate technical and organizational measures by establishing classification-based handling rules that protect PII according to its sensitivity.

## 5. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy is relevant because it establishes the organization's commitment to compliance with all applicable laws and regulations, including those related to privacy and PII protection such as GDPR. The policy ensures that Smart4Aviation operates within appropriate legal boundaries and monitors compliance with applicable laws in the Netherlands, Poland, and the European Union, directly supporting the control's purpose of ensuring compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to the information security aspects of PII protection. It provides the framework for understanding and meeting legal obligations regarding privacy and PII protection.
