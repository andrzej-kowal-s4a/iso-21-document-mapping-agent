# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.27 Learning from information security incidents

## 1. Security Incident Reporting Procedure / Procedura zgłaszania incydentów związanych z bezpieczeństwem
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure establishes the formal mechanism for reporting and tracking security incidents through Jira tickets, which is essential for collecting data about incident types and volumes as required by the control. It provides the foundation for incident documentation that enables systematic learning and analysis.

## 2. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure details the process for managing major incidents including Root Cause Analysis (RCA) requirements, which directly supports the control's requirement to identify causes of serious incidents. The procedure ensures lessons learned are captured and future preventive actions are identified and communicated.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the Security Incident Management Plan framework and commits the organization to continuous improvement of the ISMS based on security incidents and audit findings. It provides the overarching governance structure for learning from incidents and implementing improved controls.

## 4. Vulnerability Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/214794244

This policy addresses the systematic evaluation, reporting, and remediation of vulnerabilities discovered through incidents or assessments. It supports learning from incidents by ensuring vulnerabilities identified during incidents are properly tracked, assessed, and resolved to prevent recurrence.

## 5. Training Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106790

This procedure implements the training framework that enables the organization to enhance user awareness based on lessons learned from security incidents, directly addressing guidance point (c) of the control regarding updating user awareness and training with examples from incidents.

## 6. Traininig Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106783

This policy establishes the strategic approach to training and awareness, including mandatory security training requirements. It ensures that lessons learned from incidents can be systematically incorporated into awareness programs to help personnel avoid similar incidents in the future.

## 7. Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170100789

This policy establishes the risk management framework that enables the organization to update risk assessments based on incident information, addressing guidance point (b) of the control. It ensures recurring or serious incidents trigger updates to risk assessments and implementation of necessary additional controls.

## 8. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy ensures that lessons learned from security incidents are incorporated into internal audit findings, remediation tracking, and management reviews. It supports continuous improvement of the ISMS by systematically evaluating the effectiveness of controls implemented following incident learnings.
