# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.20 - Addressing information security within supplier agreements

## 1. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy is the primary document directly addressing the control requirements. It establishes comprehensive procedures for supplier security risk assessment, including RFI processes, contract negotiations with security clauses, supplier self-assessments, and ongoing monitoring of high-risk suppliers. It includes detailed security requirements covering breach notification, compliance, confidentiality, data security, disaster recovery, incident response, and supply chain security.

## 2. Company Managed Service Agreements
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108066

This standard defines the framework for Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and Operational Level Agreements (OLAs) with external parties. It establishes how to formalize service expectations and obligations between the organization and suppliers, which is essential for maintaining agreed levels of information security in supplier relationships as required by the control.

## 3. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is critical for supplier agreements as it defines how information should be classified according to sensitivity levels (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable). This classification scheme is referenced in Control 5.20 guidance (items b and c) and must be communicated to suppliers to ensure proper handling of shared information.

## 4. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy ensures compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including contractual obligations with suppliers. It addresses legal, statutory, and regulatory requirements that must be included in supplier agreements as specified in Control 5.20 guidance item (d), particularly regarding data protection and intellectual property rights.

## 5. Personal Data Protection Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101280

This policy is essential for supplier agreements involving personal data processing. It defines GDPR compliance requirements, data processing obligations, and security measures that must be addressed in supplier contracts through Data Processing Agreements (DPAs), directly supporting Control 5.20 guidance item (d) regarding handling of personally identifiable information.

## 6. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure establishes the framework for incident notification and collaboration during incident remediation, which directly addresses Control 5.20 guidance item (j). It defines how security incidents are reported and managed, providing the basis for including incident management requirements in supplier agreements.

## 7. Client contract termination checklist - customer offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160436260

This procedure addresses Control 5.20 guidance items (x) and (z) regarding termination clauses, return of assets, secure disposal of information, and handover support. It provides a practical framework for what should be included in supplier agreements regarding contract conclusion and ensures proper offboarding procedures are followed.
