# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.22 Monitoring, review and change management of supplier services

## 1. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy is the most critical document for this control as it establishes comprehensive processes for supplier security management including periodic audits of high-risk suppliers through self-assessments, monitoring changes in supplier security practices, reviewing supplier service delivery, and managing supplier relationships throughout the lifecycle from onboarding to offboarding.

## 2. Company Managed Service Agreements
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108066

This standard defines Service Level Agreements (SLA) and Operational Level Agreements (OLA) which are essential for monitoring and reviewing supplier service performance levels and ensuring compliance with agreed terms, directly supporting the control's requirement to maintain agreed levels of information security and service delivery.

## 3. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy establishes the framework for conducting internal audits of the Information Security Management System, which can include audits of supplier relationships and controls. It provides the structure for reviewing compliance with ISO 27001 requirements including supplier-related controls and managing audit findings through remediation tracking.

## 4. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy provides a comprehensive framework for managing changes to IT services and infrastructure, including changes made by suppliers or third-party providers. It ensures that supplier-related changes are properly evaluated, approved, documented, and reviewed, which is essential for monitoring changes in supplier services as required by the control.

## 5. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure establishes the process for reporting and managing security incidents, including those related to supplier services. It ensures that information security incidents involving suppliers are properly identified, reported, and managed according to agreements, which is a key requirement of control 5.22.

## 6. Vulnerability Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/214794244

This policy describes the approach to identifying, evaluating, and managing vulnerabilities across systems and services, including those provided by suppliers. It supports the control's requirement to monitor and manage information security vulnerabilities in supplier services and ensure appropriate remediation actions are taken.

## 7. Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170100789

This policy establishes the framework for identifying, analyzing, and managing risks including those related to supplier relationships. It provides the methodology for integrating supplier-related risks into decision-making processes and ensures ongoing monitoring and review of risks associated with supplier services and dependencies.
