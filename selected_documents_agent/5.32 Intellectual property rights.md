# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.32 Intellectual property rights

## 1. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure directly addresses control requirement (g) by establishing a systematic process for auditing software installed on company devices to ensure compliance with security restrictions and ISO 27001 standards. It includes verification against approved/prohibited software lists, handling of unauthorized software, and regular monthly audits to ensure only authorized and licensed products are installed.

## 2. S4A License Tracking and Update Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104620

This procedure comprehensively addresses multiple aspects of the control including maintaining license ownership records (requirement e), ensuring license limits are not exceeded (requirement f), and providing procedures for maintaining appropriate license conditions (requirement h). It establishes a complete lifecycle for license tracking, renewal, and proper management of Smart4Aviation's intellectual property.

## 3. License and hardware purchase or renewal process
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520415

This procedure directly supports requirements (c), (e), and (h) by establishing a formal process for acquiring software through proper channels, maintaining records of license ownership, and ensuring appropriate approval and tracking mechanisms are in place. It ensures software is acquired only through known and reputable sources with proper documentation.

## 4. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy establishes the foundation for ensuring compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to intellectual property rights as required by the control's purpose. It defines Smart4Aviation's commitment to comply with applicable laws and regulations including those related to copyright, licensing, and intellectual property.

## 5. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy addresses control requirements by establishing rules for appropriate use of Smart4Aviation's IT resources, including prohibitions against copyright infringement, unauthorized software installation (requirement c and g), and procedures for obtaining authorized software. It covers requirements (j) and (k) regarding compliance with terms and conditions for software obtained from external sources.

## 6. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy is relevant to requirement (c) regarding acquiring software through known and reputable sources, and requirement (j) about complying with terms and conditions for software from outside sources. It establishes procedures for evaluating third-party suppliers and their security practices, including verification of licensing compliance and intellectual property protections.

## 7. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security framework that includes IT asset management requirements (requirement d) for maintaining appropriate asset registers with intellectual property requirements. It defines responsibilities for protection of information assets including software, and establishes requirements for secure software development lifecycle that protects Smart4Aviation's own intellectual property.
