# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.13 Labelling of Information

## 1. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is fundamental to the labelling control as it establishes the classification scheme (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) that must be reflected in information labels according to ISO 27001 control 5.12 and 5.13. It defines specific sensitivity levels, handling rules, and information categories that serve as the basis for all labelling procedures.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy addresses information classification and handling requirements, including provisions that information must be classified based on confidentiality, integrity and availability, and handled securely according to its classification level. It provides the overarching framework within which information labelling procedures must operate.

## 3. Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpiecze≈Ñstwa Danych Osobowych)
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101280

This policy specifically addresses the identification, classification, and protection requirements for personal data, which must be labelled with at least "Private" level classification. It establishes data processing registers and procedures that require proper identification and categorization of personal data files, directly supporting labelling requirements.

## 4. Training Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106790

The ISO 27001 control explicitly requires that personnel be made aware of labelling procedures and provided with necessary training to ensure information is correctly labelled and handled. This procedure establishes the framework for delivering security awareness training to all team members.

## 5. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy requires users to handle information according to its sensitivity level as outlined in the Information Classification Policy, ensuring that personnel understand their responsibilities for properly treating classified information. It links classification schemes to daily operational practices and user responsibilities for information handling.
