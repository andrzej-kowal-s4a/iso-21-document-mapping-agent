# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.10 Storage media

## 1. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy directly addresses the disposal and sanitization requirements for storage media as specified in ISO 27001 Control 7.10. It establishes procedures for secure disposal of hard drives, diskettes, CDs, hard copies, and other media containing classified and sensitive data, ensuring information is properly cleared before media leaves organizational control.

## 2. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure implements the secure disposal process for electronic hardware and storage devices. It mandates destruction of data storage media before utilization to prevent data recovery, directly supporting the control's requirements for secure reuse and disposal of storage media throughout their lifecycle.

## 3. Encryption and Cryptography Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170105815

This policy addresses the control's guidance on using cryptographic techniques to protect information on removable storage media. It mandates full disk encryption for all devices, encryption of data at rest and in transit, and establishes encryption standards for protecting confidential information on storage media.

## 4. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy defines the organization's classification scheme and handling requirements referenced in Control 7.10. It establishes sensitivity levels (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) and specifies how storage media should be managed according to information classification throughout the media lifecycle.

## 5. Mobile Device Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106965

This policy governs the management of mobile storage devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops) used to access organizational data. It addresses authentication, auto-locking, physical security controls, and secure handling of mobile storage media aligned with the control's requirements for removable storage media management.

## 6. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106633

This policy covers the management of personal devices used as storage media for organizational data. It addresses encryption requirements, prohibition of storing Secret/Restricted data on BYOD devices, incident reporting for lost/stolen devices, and secure handling of removable storage media throughout their use.

## 7. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This procedure ensures proper management of storage media at the end of the employee lifecycle. It covers hardware recovery, data removal, account deactivation, and return of all storage devices, supporting the control's requirement for managing storage media through their complete lifecycle including disposal.

## 8. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall framework for protecting information assets, including storage media. It defines IT asset management, physical security requirements, access management, and responsibilities for handling storage media according to classification levels throughout their lifecycle of acquisition, use, transportation, and disposal.
