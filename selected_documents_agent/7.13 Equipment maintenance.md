# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.13 Equipment maintenance

## 1. Server management procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure directly addresses equipment maintenance for servers and virtual machines, including resource management, environment verification, lifecycle management, and decommissioning processes. It establishes controls for authorized maintenance personnel and maintaining records of equipment status throughout its lifecycle.

## 2. Hardware insurance list procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/334365211

This procedure supports the control requirement to comply with insurance maintenance requirements (guidance point i). It establishes a systematic approach to tracking insured equipment, documenting hardware details including purchase dates, replacement values, and maintaining up-to-date records of all equipment that requires insurance coverage.

## 3. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure addresses guidance point k by establishing measures for secure disposal of equipment. It includes authorization requirements through an utilization committee, proper documentation protocols, and requirements for data destruction on storage devices before disposal to prevent information compromise.

## 4. License and hardware purchase or renewal process
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520415

This procedure supports maintaining equipment according to specifications (guidance point a) by establishing a controlled process for hardware and license acquisition, renewal, and management. It ensures proper authorization and approval processes for equipment procurement and maintenance activities.

## 5. Purchasing Procedure for Standard Hardware
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1633452033

This procedure establishes equipment lifecycle management including standard lifespans (5 years for laptops, 8 years for monitors), replacement procedures, and specifications. It supports guidance points a and d by ensuring equipment is maintained according to specifications and defining when equipment should be replaced to prevent failures.

## 6. Maintenance windows (BT) - working procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102577

This procedure addresses guidance point e by implementing appropriate controls when equipment is scheduled for maintenance, including authorization requirements, change approval processes, validation procedures, and monitoring after maintenance to ensure equipment is functioning properly.

## 7. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This procedure supports physical security aspects of equipment maintenance by establishing controls for hardware recovery during employee departure, ensuring proper documentation and tracking of equipment assignments, and preventing unauthorized access to equipment and associated information assets.
