# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.16 Identity Management

## 1. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure directly addresses the identity establishment phase of the lifecycle by detailing how new identities are created and configured for employees and contractors. It covers identity creation in LDAP, assignment to appropriate groups, verification of identity before creating logical identities, and the initial setup of authentication services - all core requirements of control 5.16.

## 2. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure is essential for identity lifecycle management as it ensures identities are disabled or removed when no longer required. It addresses the timely removal of access rights, recovery of credentials, and revocation of privileges when employees leave - directly supporting the control's requirement for managing identity termination.

## 3. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This technical procedure provides detailed tasks for identity deactivation including LDAP account deactivation, removal from groups, Google account suspension, Jira deactivation, and removal from various systems. It ensures comprehensive identity removal across all systems, supporting the control's requirement for complete identity lifecycle management.

## 4. Gerrit - access request
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099777

This procedure demonstrates proper identity and access management practices including approval workflows for granting access rights, proper authorization processes, and documented access requests. It supports the control's requirement for appropriate assignment of access rights and segregated approval for identity-related privileges.

## 5. Accounts for Customers in Jira/Confluence - Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099448

This policy addresses identity lifecycle management for external users, including periodic review of account usage, decommissioning of inactive accounts, and limits on identity provisioning. It ensures that identities for third parties are managed appropriately and unused identities are deactivated - supporting control 5.16's requirement for managing the full lifecycle of identities.

## 6. Google accounts howto create
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/496828547

This procedure covers the creation and management of identities using third-party identity providers (Google), directly addressing the control's guidance on using identities provided by third parties. It ensures proper trust level verification and risk management when using external identity services as required by control 5.16.

## 7. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This overarching policy establishes the framework for access management and identity management across the organization. It defines requirements for unique user identifiers, authentication methods, access privilege management, and the principle of least privilege - all fundamental to supporting ISO 27001 control 5.16 on identity management.

## 8. Confluence Pages Access Request Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/876347426

This procedure demonstrates proper authorization and access control processes for specific resources, showing how access rights are requested, approved, and granted. It supports the control's requirement for appropriate assignment of access rights and maintaining accountability through documented access request procedures.
