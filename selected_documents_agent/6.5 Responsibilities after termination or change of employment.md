# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 6.5 Responsibilities after termination or change of employment

## 1. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odej≈õcia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure directly addresses the core requirements of the control by defining the structured offboarding process, including knowledge transfer, asset recovery, access revocation, and communication of ongoing responsibilities during the notice period. It ensures information security responsibilities are properly managed through the termination process.

## 2. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This technical procedure provides detailed IT and security-focused offboarding tasks including LDAP deactivation, access revocation, hardware recovery, license management, and removal from all systems. It ensures that information security controls are properly enforced when employment terminates and access rights are systematically removed.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security responsibility framework including personnel security requirements, access management principles, and the requirement for proper offboarding procedures. It defines security responsibilities that apply throughout the employment lifecycle including separation, supporting the control's objectives for ongoing security obligations.

## 4. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy defines information sensitivity levels and handling requirements that remain applicable after employment ends, particularly regarding confidentiality obligations. It supports the control by clarifying which information must remain protected and how employees must handle confidential information even after termination.

## 5. Personnel Vetting Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104805

This policy establishes the framework for personnel screening and security requirements throughout the employment lifecycle. It defines responsibilities related to information handling and security obligations that are established at the beginning of employment and continue through termination, supporting the control's requirement to define ongoing responsibilities.

## 6. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy defines responsibilities for appropriate use of IT resources including data protection, confidentiality, and return of equipment upon offboarding. It establishes security obligations that personnel must understand and maintain, including restrictions that continue after employment ends regarding confidential information and intellectual property.
