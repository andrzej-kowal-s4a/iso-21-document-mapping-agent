# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.11 Return of Assets

## 1. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odej≈õcia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure directly addresses the return of assets during employee termination by defining a 10-step offboarding process. It specifically covers transfer of knowledge, recovery of organization assets, revocation of privileges and access, and completion of employee offboarding checklists, which are core requirements of Control 5.11.

## 2. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This detailed technical procedure provides comprehensive step-by-step instructions for IT Helpdesk to recover hardware, deactivate accounts, revoke access, and reassign licenses during employee separation. It includes specific tasks for removing LDAP accounts, Google accounts, hardware collection, license management, and revoking customer access grants, directly implementing the asset return requirements of Control 5.11.

## 3. Mobile Device Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106965

This policy governs corporate and private mobile devices used to access company data. It requires users to report damage or loss of mobile devices storing company data as security incidents and establishes rules for device management and control, supporting the asset protection objectives of Control 5.11, particularly for mobile endpoint devices.

## 4. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106633

This policy addresses the use of personal devices for work purposes and includes provisions for security incident handling, remote wiping of company data from private devices, and ensuring data is returned when BYOD devices are used. This is particularly relevant to Control 5.11's guidance on handling assets not owned by the organization and preventing unauthorized copying of information.

## 5. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure establishes the formal process for disposing of electronic hardware, including data sanitization requirements before disposal or re-use. It complements Control 5.11 by ensuring that when assets are returned, they are properly handled and any sensitive information is securely deleted before equipment disposal or reallocation.

## 6. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy defines requirements for proper disposal and sanitization of media containing classified and sensitive data. It supports Control 5.11's requirement to securely delete information from equipment and ensure organization assets are properly cleared when returned or transferred, protecting confidentiality of organizational information.

## 7. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure establishes systematic auditing of software installed on company devices, ensuring compliance with security restrictions. It supports Control 5.11 by providing visibility and control over what software and data resides on organizational assets, helping track what needs to be returned or removed during termination processes.

## 8. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security framework including IT asset management, personnel security, and the requirement for processes to ensure information and IT assets are returned prior to employee separation. It provides the high-level policy foundation that supports implementation of Control 5.11 across the organization.
