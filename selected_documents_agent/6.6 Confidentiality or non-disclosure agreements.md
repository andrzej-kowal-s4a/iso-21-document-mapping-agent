# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 6.6 Confidentiality or non-disclosure agreements

## 1. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy directly addresses confidentiality and non-disclosure requirements for suppliers and external parties. It includes specific security requirements for confidentiality clauses in contracts, template contractual security clauses, and establishes procedures for ensuring suppliers sign and comply with confidentiality agreements. The policy explicitly covers NDA (Non-Disclosure Agreement) requirements and when they should be signed with external parties.

## 2. Personnel Vetting Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104805

This policy covers personnel security verification and identity verification procedures for employees and contractors. It establishes requirements for personnel screening and verification, which are essential components of personnel security that work alongside confidentiality agreements. The policy addresses how the organization verifies the identity of personnel who will have access to confidential information.

## 3. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure details the onboarding process where new employees and contractors are required to sign GDPR documents, confidentiality agreements, and other mandatory HR-related documents through BambooHR. It ensures that confidentiality obligations are established at the start of the employment relationship and that personnel are aware of their responsibilities to protect information.

## 4. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure addresses the termination phase of employment, including the management of confidentiality obligations after termination. It covers the return of confidential information, revocation of access, and ensures that departing personnel understand their ongoing confidentiality obligations. This is critical as Control 6.6 specifically mentions "required actions when an agreement is terminated."

## 5. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy defines information sensitivity levels and establishes which types of information require confidentiality protection. It explicitly maps confidentiality clause coverage to different information sensitivity levels (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) and confirms that confidentiality clauses signed by employees and contractors cover specific categories of information, directly supporting Control 6.6's requirement to define information to be protected.

## 6. Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpieczeństwa Danych Osobowych)
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101280

This policy establishes confidentiality requirements for personal data processing and includes provisions for data processing agreements with external parties. It addresses confidentiality obligations under GDPR, defines responsibilities for protecting confidential personal data, and covers data processing agreements which function as specialized forms of confidentiality agreements for personal data.

## 7. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall framework for information security, including responsibilities for protecting confidential information and maintaining confidentiality of customer and corporate data. It defines security responsibilities and obligations that underpin confidentiality requirements and supports the enforcement of confidentiality agreements across the organization.

## 8. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy defines user responsibilities for protecting confidential information when using IT resources. It reinforces confidentiality obligations by establishing acceptable use rules, prohibiting unauthorized disclosure, and requiring users to handle information according to its sensitivity level. This supports the practical implementation of confidentiality agreements in daily operations.
