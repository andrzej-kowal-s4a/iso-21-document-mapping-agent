# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.31 Legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements

## 1. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy is directly aligned with Control 5.31 as it establishes the framework for identifying, documenting, and maintaining compliance with legal and regulatory obligations across Smart4Aviation Group. It defines responsibilities for monitoring applicable laws and regulations in the Netherlands, Poland, and the EU, and establishes processes for ensuring compliance with legal boundaries relevant to information security.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy is essential for Control 5.31 as it defines mandatory minimum security requirements that ensure compliance with legal, statutory, and regulatory requirements. It addresses privacy, personal data processing requirements (GDPR), and ensures that all security policies and standards across the organization are designed to meet external compliance obligations while supporting secure IT infrastructure.

## 4. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy supports Control 5.31 by ensuring contractual security requirements are properly identified and managed with suppliers and vendors. It includes template contractual security clauses, defines security requirements for supplier contracts, and ensures that third-party relationships meet legal and regulatory compliance standards for information security.

## 5. Encryption and Cryptography Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170105815

This policy directly addresses the cryptography guidance section of Control 5.31, which specifies that compliance with agreements, laws, and regulations relating to cryptographic functions must be considered. It establishes standards for encryption that comply with legal requirements, including restrictions on import/export of cryptographic technology and ensures alignment with regulatory frameworks.

## 6. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure is essential for Control 5.31 compliance as it operationalizes the statutory requirements under GDPR for breach notification to supervisory authorities and data subjects. It defines the process for meeting the legal obligation to report breaches within 72 hours and ensures compliance with data protection regulations across the EU.

## 7. Company Managed Service Agreements
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108066

This standard supports Control 5.31 by documenting the approach to contractual requirements related to information security through Service Level Agreements (SLAs) and Operational Level Agreements (OLAs).
