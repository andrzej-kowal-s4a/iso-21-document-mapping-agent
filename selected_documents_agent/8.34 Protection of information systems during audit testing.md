# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 8.34 Protection of information systems during audit testing

## 1. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy directly establishes the framework for planning, conducting, and managing internal audits of the ISMS, ensuring audits evaluate conformity with ISO 27001. It defines audit program requirements, competence of auditors, objectivity principles, and guidelines for evidence gathering, reporting, and follow-up that align with the control's purpose of minimizing audit impact on operational systems.

## 2. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure outlines systematic software auditing on company devices with clear notification processes, data submission requirements, and reporting mechanisms. It demonstrates practical implementation of audit testing controls including defined audit frequency, selection criteria, and non-compliance handling that protects operational systems during assessment activities.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy defines security objectives, access management principles, and separation of duties requirements that are essential for controlling audit access to systems and data. It establishes the foundation for security controls during audit activities including access privileges, authentication requirements, and logging obligations mentioned in the control guidance.

## 4. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy defines formal processes for making changes to IT systems, including evaluation, approval, and documentation requirements. It ensures that any audit-related changes or test activities follow structured processes to minimize disruption to operational systems, directly supporting the control's objective of controlled and planned audit activities.

## 5. Vulnerability Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/214794244

This policy describes the approach to vulnerability identification, evaluation, and treatment including testing procedures for systems. It establishes frameworks for conducting security assessments and handling findings through Jira workflows, ensuring vulnerability testing activities are properly managed and documented to avoid operational impact.

## 6. Server Management Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure defines comprehensive lifecycle management for virtual machines including resource allocation, approval workflows, and environment preparation. It ensures that any test or audit environments are properly provisioned and controlled, supporting the control's guidance on using isolated copies of system files and controlling access during audit activities.
