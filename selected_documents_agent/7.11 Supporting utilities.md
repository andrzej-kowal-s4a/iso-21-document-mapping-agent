# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.11 Supporting utilities

## 1. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This document is directly relevant to Control 7.11 as it addresses how the organization responds to disruptions and failures affecting information processing facilities. It provides procedures for maintaining operations and recovering from utility failures that could impact business continuity and service availability.

## 2. New NAGIOS monitoring request procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104450

This procedure establishes monitoring and alerting mechanisms to detect utility malfunctions and system failures in real-time, which directly supports Control 7.11's requirement to raise alarms to detect utilities malfunctions and ensure proper functioning through regular inspection and testing.

## 3. Server management procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure covers the lifecycle management of servers including resource requirements, capacity planning, and maintenance activities. It supports Control 7.11 requirements for ensuring equipment supporting utilities is configured, operated, and maintained according to specifications and regularly inspected for proper functioning.

## 4. Alarm codes management
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520699

This procedure manages physical security alarm systems for office facilities, including emergency access controls. It relates to Control 7.11's guidance on emergency switches, valves, and physical security measures that protect information processing facilities from environmental threats.

## 5. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure ensures that incidents affecting the availability and integrity of information processing facilities are promptly reported and managed, supporting Control 7.11's objectives to prevent loss, damage, or interruption to operations due to utility failures.

## 6. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure defines the response to major incidents that affect service availability, including those caused by infrastructure or utility failures. It provides communication protocols and escalation procedures essential for responding to disruptions mentioned in Control 7.11.

## 7. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes overall security objectives including ensuring system availability, business continuity, and physical security. It provides the policy framework that supports all controls including 7.11's requirements for protecting facilities from utility disruptions and maintaining availability.
