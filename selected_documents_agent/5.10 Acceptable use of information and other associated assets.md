# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.10 Acceptable use of information and other associated assets

## 1. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy directly addresses the control requirements by establishing comprehensive rules for acceptable use of Smart4Aviation's information technology resources. It defines permitted and prohibited uses, expected behaviors, monitoring activities, and procedures for handling information across its full lifecycle including authentication, data protection, communication, internet use, and physical security requirements.

## 2. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is essential for the control as it establishes the information classification scheme (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) and defines specific handling rules for each classification level. It ensures that information and assets are protected according to their sensitivity level, which directly supports the acceptable use requirements in Control 5.10.

## 3. Mobile Device Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106965

This policy governs the acceptable use of mobile devices (smartphones, tablets, laptops) used by staff to access company information. It establishes security requirements, handling procedures, configuration standards, and terms of use that align with the control's requirements for proper handling and protection of information assets throughout their lifecycle.

## 4. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106633

This policy establishes acceptable use rules for personal devices accessing company information and systems. It defines permitted usage patterns, security requirements, data handling restrictions, and compliance obligations that ensure information assets are appropriately protected and handled when accessed through private devices.

## 5. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy addresses the disposal phase of the information lifecycle as required by Control 5.10. It outlines proper disposal/sanitization/destruction procedures for media containing sensitive information, ensuring authorized deletion methods are followed and information remains protected until final disposal.

## 6. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure provides detailed steps for disposing of electronic hardware and ensures data is properly sanitized before disposal. It directly supports Control 5.10's requirement (item f) for authorization of disposal of information and assets and supported deletion methods, including committee oversight and proper documentation.

## 7. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure establishes systematic monitoring of software installed on company devices to ensure compliance with acceptable use policies. It implements regular audits comparing installed software against approved/prohibited lists, directly supporting Control 5.10's requirement for monitoring activities and enforcement of acceptable use rules.

## 8. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security framework including information classification, asset management, access management, and security responsibilities. It provides the foundational requirements that support acceptable use policies and ensures information assets are appropriately protected, used, and handled throughout their lifecycle as required by Control 5.10.
