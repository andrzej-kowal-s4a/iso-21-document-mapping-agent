# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.8 Equipment siting and protection

## 1. Office Alarm Code
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107751

This procedure directly addresses physical security controls for office access, including alarm management and authorized entry procedures. It defines how equipment and facilities are protected through controlled access systems, which is essential for minimizing unauthorized access to work areas where equipment is located.

## 2. Alarm codes management
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520699

This procedure provides detailed technical guidance on managing alarm codes and access control systems for physical security. It directly supports the control's requirement to minimize unauthorized access by defining how alarm systems are configured and maintained to protect equipment and facilities.

## 3. After-Hours workplace activity procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104529

This bilingual procedure addresses physical security during non-standard hours, ensuring equipment and facilities remain protected when normal security measures may be relaxed. It covers safety and security of staff, facilities, and equipment after hours, directly supporting the control's objective to reduce risks from unauthorized access and damage.

## 4. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy includes a dedicated Physical Security section that mandates security perimeters, barriers, and access controls for information processing facilities. It requires that equipment be protected from security threats and environmental hazards, directly aligning with the control's requirements for equipment siting and protection.

## 5. Server management procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure addresses the management and protection of server equipment including physical server assignment, environment specifications, and resource management. It ensures servers are properly managed and positioned, supporting the control's requirement for secure equipment siting and protection of critical IT infrastructure.

## 6. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This plan addresses protection against environmental threats and ensures business continuity in case of disasters affecting equipment. It directly supports the control's guidance on minimizing risks from physical and environmental threats such as fire, water, and other hazards that could damage equipment.

## 7. Hardware insurance list procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/334365211

This procedure maintains an inventory of insured hardware equipment, demonstrating asset management and protection measures. It supports the control by ensuring valuable equipment is tracked, insured against theft and damage, and properly documented for protection purposes.

## 8. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure ensures secure disposal and decommissioning of equipment, including data sanitization requirements. It addresses the control's requirement to protect equipment throughout its lifecycle and prevent information leakage, theft, or unauthorized access during disposal processes.
