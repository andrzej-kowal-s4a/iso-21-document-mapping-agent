# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.18 Access rights

## 1. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure directly addresses the revocation of access rights when employees leave the organization, which is a critical requirement of Control 5.18. It covers the systematic removal of privileges, recovery of assets, and transfer of responsibilities during termination or change of employment, ensuring timely revocation of access rights as required by the control guidance.

## 2. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This detailed technical procedure implements the specific steps for revoking access rights across all systems (LDAP, Google, Jira, Slack, VPN, etc.). It ensures comprehensive removal of both logical and physical access rights on the last working day, maintaining central records of access rights removal, which directly supports Control 5.18 requirements for documented access revocation processes.

## 3. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure establishes the provisioning process for access rights when new employees join, including authorization requirements, account creation, and group assignments. It addresses Control 5.18 requirements for proper authorization before granting access and maintaining records of access rights provisioned to users throughout the onboarding process.

## 4. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella security policy establishes the organizational framework for access management, including access control principles, least privilege, role-based authorization, segregation of duties, and the requirement for individual user identifiers. It provides the topic-specific policy foundation that Control 5.18 references for access control rules and governance.

## 5. Gerrit - access request
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099777

This procedure defines the formal access request and approval process for Gerrit repository access, demonstrating the implementation of authorization workflows. It ensures that access rights are granted only after proper approval from management/asset owners, maintaining records of access requests and approvals as required by Control 5.18.

## 6. Confluence Pages Access Request Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/876347426

This procedure establishes the process for requesting and approving access to Confluence pages, ensuring that access rights to information assets are properly authorized by page/space owners before being granted. It supports Control 5.18's requirement for obtaining authorization from information asset owners before provisioning access.

## 7. Accounts for Customers in Jira/Confluence - Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099448

This policy addresses the regular review and decommissioning of user accounts based on activity monitoring, specifically requiring review of accounts inactive for more than 3 months. It directly implements Control 5.18's guidance on periodic review of access rights and timely removal of access when no longer needed.

## 8. Google accounts howto create
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/496828547

This procedure documents the process for provisioning Google accounts with appropriate roles and group memberships, including the authorization requirements and role assignments. It demonstrates the implementation of access provisioning controls and maintains consistency with Control 5.18 requirements for documented access granting procedures.
