# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 8.10 Information deletion

## 1. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy directly addresses the disposal and sanitization of media containing sensitive information. It establishes rules for proper disposal of hard drives, diskettes, CDs, and other media that process, store, or transmit classified and sensitive data, ensuring information is cleared before releasing equipment from organizational control.

## 2. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure provides specific operational steps for disposal of electronic hardware, including the requirement to destroy data storage devices (hard drives) before utilization to prevent data recovery. It references sanitization procedures and establishes a utilization committee process with documentation requirements.

## 3. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This procedure includes comprehensive steps for removing employee access to systems, revoking accounts, and ensuring data deletion when employees leave. It addresses information deletion in LDAP, Google accounts, JIRA, licenses, and other systems, ensuring no unauthorized data retention after employment ends.

## 4. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure establishes the organizational framework for employee offboarding, including knowledge transfer, asset recovery, revocation of privileges and access, and informing customers about access removal. It supports the control by ensuring systematic deletion of access rights and information when employees depart.

## 5. Client contract termination checklist - customer offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160436260

This procedure addresses information deletion when customer contracts terminate, including disabling VPN access, bringing down environments, performing encrypted backups if required, and destroying all customer data with reference to sanitization procedures. It implements the 21-day process for complete data destruction.

## 6. Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpieczeństwa Danych Osobowych)
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101280

This policy addresses personal data retention periods and ensures compliance with GDPR requirements for data deletion. It establishes that data should only be kept as long as necessary to achieve processing purposes, supporting the legal and regulatory compliance aspects of the information deletion control.

## 7. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy defines information sensitivity levels and establishes rules for handling different types of information, including deletion requirements based on classification. It provides the foundation for determining which information requires secure deletion methods and when information should be deleted based on its sensitivity.

## 8. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy addresses supplier off-boarding and the requirement to retrieve all organizational information from suppliers when contracts expire or terminate. It ensures that third parties who store organization information delete it appropriately upon termination of services, supporting the control's guidance on third-party information deletion.
