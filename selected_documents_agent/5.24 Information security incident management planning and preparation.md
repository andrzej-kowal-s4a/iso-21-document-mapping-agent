# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.24 Information security incident management planning and preparation

## 1. Security Incident Reporting Procedure / Procedura zgłaszania incydentów związanych z bezpieczeństwem
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure directly addresses the control requirement for establishing a common method for reporting information security events including point of contact. It defines roles, responsibilities, and a clear reporting process using Jira (SECURITY project) for managing security incidents, which is fundamental to incident management planning and preparation.

## 2. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure establishes the incident response process for managing major incidents, including roles and responsibilities, escalation procedures, communication protocols, and notification requirements. It directly supports the control's requirement for incident management processes covering detection, triage, prioritization, analysis, communication, and coordinating interested parties.

## 3. Communication procedure in case of Major Incident
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102638

This procedure specifically addresses the communication requirements during major incidents, detailing the process flow from notification to closure. It establishes clear communication channels and responsibilities for both internal and external interested parties, which is a key aspect of incident management planning required by the control.

## 4. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure addresses a specific category of security incidents - personal data breaches. It defines breach detection, assessment, notification procedures, and timelines for reporting to supervisory authorities and data subjects, supporting the control's requirements for incident response processes and external communication obligations.

## 5. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This policy establishes the overarching framework for security incident management within the organization's ISMS. It defines security responsibilities, incident management commitments, and links to supporting procedures, providing the governance structure required for incident management planning and preparation.

## 6. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This plan addresses the recovery aspects of incident management, establishing procedures for responding to and recovering from significant incidents or disasters. It supports the control's requirements for incident response capability and coordinating recovery activities, which are essential components of incident management preparation.

## 7. Procedure - HowTo propagate information about major issue found in product
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102266

This procedure establishes the emergency patching process for proactive incident management when critical issues are discovered in products. It defines roles, decision-making processes, and communication protocols for responding to potential incidents before they impact customers, supporting the control's requirements for quick, effective response to information security events.

## 8. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy establishes structured change management processes that help prevent incidents through proper planning and controlled implementation of changes. It supports incident prevention and defines responsibilities and workflows that complement incident management processes, contributing to overall incident management planning and preparation.
