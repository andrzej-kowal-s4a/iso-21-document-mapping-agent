# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.30 ICT readiness for business continuity

## 1. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This is the primary document directly addressing ICT continuity and disaster recovery. It establishes the framework for recovery procedures, which is essential for meeting the control's requirements for ICT continuity plans, recovery time objectives (RTO), and recovery point objectives (RPO) for ICT services.

## 2. Procedure - Service transition to prod
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433255

This comprehensive procedure ensures ICT services are properly prepared for production with critical continuity elements including DR procedure requirements, backup testing, monitoring setup, and Business Continuity Plan (BCP) testing. It directly supports the control's requirement for tested ICT continuity plans and organizational structure for disruption response.

## 3. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure defines the response process for major ICT service disruptions, including roles, responsibilities, and escalation procedures. It directly addresses the control's requirement for response and recovery procedures when ICT services are disrupted and ensures proper competence and authority during incidents.

## 4. Communication procedure in case of Major Incident
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102638

This procedure establishes structured communication protocols during ICT service disruptions, ensuring coordination between teams and customers. It supports the control's requirement for organizational structure and competence to respond to disruptions and manage recovery activities.

## 5. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy governs all changes to IT services and infrastructure, ensuring changes are planned, tested, and reviewed to minimize disruption. It supports the control's requirement for maintaining ICT readiness and ensuring changes don't negatively impact continuity capabilities.

## 6. New NAGIOS monitoring request procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104450

This procedure establishes monitoring for ICT services to detect issues before they cause disruptions. It supports the control's requirement to respond before disruption occurs and ensures proper reaction times and responsibilities are defined for detecting ICT service issues.

## 7. Server management procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure manages ICT infrastructure resources including lifecycle management, capacity planning, and resource allocation. It supports the control's requirement for ensuring adequate organizational structure and resources are available to support ICT services during normal operations and disruptions.

## 8. Maintenance windows (BT) - working procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102577

This procedure defines planned maintenance activities including deployment, testing, and validation processes to minimize service disruption. It supports the control's requirement for maintaining ICT services and testing recovery procedures in a controlled manner.
