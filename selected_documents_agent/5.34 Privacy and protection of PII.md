This is the primary policy document directly addressing privacy and PII protection requirements. ## 2. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure provides essential operational guidance for detecting, reporting, and responding to personal data breaches as required by GDPR Article 33-34. It includes breach classification, risk assessment methodology, notification procedures to supervisory authorities and data subjects, and documentation requirements - critical elements for maintaining PII protection and regulatory compliance.

## 3. Regulamin ochrony danych osobowych_Rules of personal data protection_BILINGUAL
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102152

This bilingual document provides detailed operational rules and procedures for all personnel handling personal data. It covers secure IT usage, password policies, physical document handling, access management, and confidentiality obligations - ensuring that privacy protection requirements are communicated to and understood by all relevant personnel as required by the control.

## 4. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy establishes the organization's commitment to comply with all applicable laws and regulations, including GDPR and privacy legislation in the Netherlands, Poland, and the EU. It defines the framework for monitoring legal obligations and ensuring contractual requirements related to PII protection are identified and met across different jurisdictions.

## 5. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella security policy explicitly addresses personal data security and privacy as key objectives. It assigns high confidentiality classification to personal data by default, requires lawful and transparent processing, mandates privacy considerations throughout the SDLC, and integrates PII protection into the overall information security management system.

## 6. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy provides the framework for classifying information based on confidentiality, integrity, and availability requirements. It ensures that PII is appropriately classified and handled with appropriate technical and organizational measures based on its sensitivity level - a fundamental requirement for protecting personally identifiable information according to applicable laws and regulations.
