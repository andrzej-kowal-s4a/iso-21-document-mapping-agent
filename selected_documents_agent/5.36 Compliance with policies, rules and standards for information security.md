# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.36 Compliance with policies, rules and standards for information security

## 1. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy directly addresses the control requirement by establishing a framework for planning, conducting, and following up on internal audits to evaluate conformity with ISO 27001 clauses, Annex A controls, and internal normative documents. It defines audit frequency, reporting of findings, tracking of corrective actions, and ensures that compliance reviews are systematically documented and monitored for continuous improvement.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This is the umbrella information security policy that establishes the overall security requirements, objectives, and mandatory minimal security standards for the organization. It defines security responsibilities, risk management, and the compliance framework that all other topic-specific policies and standards must align with, making it essential for understanding what must be reviewed for compliance under this control.

## 3. Policy, Standards, and Procedures Exceptions Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107067

This procedure establishes the formal process for managing deviations from policies, standards, and procedures. It ensures that any non-compliance is properly reviewed, evaluated for risk, approved by appropriate authorities, and periodically reviewed - directly supporting the control's requirement to identify and address non-compliance with established policies and standards.

## 4. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure provides a concrete example of regular compliance review by systematically auditing software installed on company devices against security restrictions and ISO 27001 standards. It demonstrates monthly periodic reviews, identification of non-compliance, corrective actions, and reporting - all key elements required by the control for ensuring compliance with organizational security policies.

## 5. Policy Owner vs Author of procedures, standards, guidances and processes
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102632

This policy defines the roles, responsibilities, and lifecycle management for all organizational policies, procedures, standards, and guidance documents. It establishes the framework for policy review cycles, measurement of outcomes, identification of non-compliance issues, and ongoing updates - essential for ensuring that the entire policy framework remains current and that compliance is regularly reviewed as required by this control.

## 6. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy establishes the organization's commitment to complying with applicable laws, regulations, and contractual obligations related to information security. It supports the control by ensuring that compliance reviews include not only internal policies and standards but also external legal and regulatory requirements that the organization must meet.
