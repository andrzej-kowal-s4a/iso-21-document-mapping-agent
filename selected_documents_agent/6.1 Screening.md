# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 6.1 Screening

## 1. Personnel Vetting Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104805

This policy directly addresses the core requirements of Control 6.1 by defining procedures for personnel identity verification, employment reference checks, and candidate screening processes. It specifies how background verification checks are conducted during onboarding, including identity-proving document verification, and addresses legal compliance with privacy laws and GDPR requirements for personnel screening activities.

## 2. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure implements the screening control during the employee onboarding process. It details the pre-arrival verification steps, identity verification requirements on the first day (showing ID card to HR), and the structured process for ensuring personnel suitability before granting access to information systems and sensitive data. It supports ongoing verification through the onboarding period.

## 3. Internal Recruitment Procedure / Procedura rekrutacji wewnętrznej
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170098836

This procedure ensures that internal candidates undergo appropriate screening and verification processes when transferring to new positions, particularly those requiring access to different classifications of information. It addresses ongoing suitability verification during employment by establishing processes for role changes and promotions within the organization.

## 4. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure supports the lifecycle management aspect of screening by ensuring proper exit processes, including revocation of access privileges, return of assets, and knowledge transfer. It complements screening requirements by ensuring that personnel who no longer meet suitability criteria or leave the organization have their access properly terminated.

## 5. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This overarching policy establishes personnel security requirements including workforce suitability determinations for positions requiring access to high confidentiality information. It sets the framework for personnel screening by requiring security awareness training and establishing the principle that personnel handling sensitive data must undergo appropriate verification before access is granted.

## 6. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy addresses the screening requirements for contracted personnel, subcontractors, and suppliers as specified in Control 6.1 guidance. It establishes security verification requirements for third-party workforce, including background checks, security certifications, and ongoing assessment of suppliers who provide personnel to work with Smart4Aviation systems and data.

## 7. Training Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106783

This policy supports the competency verification aspect of screening by establishing mandatory training requirements and ensuring personnel have necessary qualifications for their roles. It addresses the control's requirement that candidates for information security roles must demonstrate necessary competence, particularly through mandatory security awareness training and role-specific development programs.
