# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.35 Independent review of information security

## 1. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy directly addresses the core requirement of Control 5.35 by establishing a comprehensive framework for conducting independent internal audits of the ISMS. It defines audit programs, competence requirements for auditors ensuring objectivity and impartiality, and establishes processes for findings management, reporting, and follow-up actionsâ€”all essential elements for independent review of information security.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall approach to managing information security across the organization, including people, processes, and technologies. It defines security objectives, responsibilities, and the continuous improvement commitment that forms the foundation for independent reviews. This document represents the information security approach that Control 5.35 requires to be independently reviewed.

## 3. Endpoint Software Audit Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1994686607

This procedure demonstrates independent review implementation by establishing a systematic, periodic audit process for software compliance on company devices. It involves the Internal Helpdesk Team conducting reviews independently and reporting to the Security Officer, exemplifying the independent review approach required by Control 5.35 for specific security controls.

## 4. Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170100789

This policy establishes processes for identifying, analyzing, and managing both operational and enterprise risks, including regular monitoring and review requirements. Independent reviews under Control 5.35 should assess the effectiveness of risk management approaches, making this policy essential for evaluating the organization's approach to managing information security risks.

## 5. Vulnerability Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/214794244

This policy describes the organization's systematic approach to identifying, evaluating, reporting, and treating vulnerabilities across software products, systems, and infrastructure. Independent reviews should assess the effectiveness of vulnerability management implementation as part of evaluating the organization's technical security controls and processes.

## 6. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy establishes formal change control procedures for all systems, particularly those affecting production environments. Independent reviews under Control 5.35 should verify that change management processes are being followed effectively, as changes represent significant events that may trigger the need for independent security reviews.
