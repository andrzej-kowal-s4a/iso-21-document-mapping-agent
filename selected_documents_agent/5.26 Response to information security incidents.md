# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.26 Response to information security incidents

## 1. Security Incident Reporting Procedure / Procedura zgłaszania incydentów związanych z bezpieczeństwem
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure is directly relevant as it establishes the formal process for reporting security incidents, creating Jira tickets, and initiating incident management by the response team (S4A CSIRT). It addresses the control's requirement for documented procedures on information security incident response and ensures incidents are properly logged.

## 2. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure defines the incident response process for major incidents including notification, escalation, involvement of responsible parties, resolution process, and root cause analysis requirements. It directly supports the control's requirements for containment, escalation, logging response activities, and post-incident analysis.

## 3. Communication procedure in case of Major Incident
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102638

This procedure specifically addresses the communication requirements outlined in the control, including notifying internal and external parties (customers, support teams, management) following the need-to-know principle, and coordinating response activities during major incidents.

## 4. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This document addresses the control's requirement for business continuity plans that may be invoked during escalation and crisis management activities. It ensures efficient recovery from incidents that could impact system availability and business operations.

## 5. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure covers incident response for a specific critical incident type (personal data breaches), including risk assessment, notification to authorities and data subjects, evidence collection, and post-incident documentation. It demonstrates compliance with specialized incident response requirements.

## 6. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This overarching policy establishes the framework for security incident management, defines security responsibilities, and mandates the Security Incident Management Plan. It provides the high-level requirements that incident response procedures must fulfill and addresses coordination with interested parties.

## 7. Vulnerability Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/214794244

This policy directly addresses the control's requirement (point j) for identifying and managing information security vulnerabilities and weaknesses, including those related to controls which have caused, contributed to or failed to prevent incidents. It defines the process for handling vulnerabilities discovered during or after incidents.

## 8. Change Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104688

This policy supports incident response by ensuring that remediation actions and changes resulting from incidents are properly controlled, documented, and implemented following a structured process. It addresses the formal closure of incidents and implementation of corrective actions to prevent recurrence.
