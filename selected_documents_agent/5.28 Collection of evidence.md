# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.28 Collection of evidence

## 1. Security Incident Reporting Procedure / Procedura zgłaszania incydentów związanych z bezpieczeństwem
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure is essential for Control 5.28 as it establishes the formal process for reporting security incidents, which is the first step in evidence collection. It defines how incidents are documented in Jira, ensuring that security events are properly identified and tracked, which forms the foundation for evidence management related to information security events.

## 2. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure directly supports Control 5.28 by establishing specific requirements for documenting personal data breaches, maintaining breach registers, and preserving evidence in SECURITY tickets. It includes detailed guidance on risk assessment, notification requirements, and documentation standards that ensure evidence is collected and preserved in accordance with legal and regulatory requirements.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security incident management framework and requirements for logging, monitoring, and responding to security events. It provides the foundational requirements for security controls that enable evidence collection, including system monitoring, access logging, and incident response procedures that are critical for Control 5.28 compliance.

## 4. Internal Auditing Policy for Security
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/2086699019

This policy supports Control 5.28 by establishing requirements for collecting, maintaining, and preserving audit evidence related to information security. It defines how findings are documented in Jira, evidence retention requirements, and the need for sufficient documented information (logs, records, artifacts) that can be used as evidence for security assessments and investigations.

## 5. Major Incidents
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/SUP/pages/160433356

This procedure is relevant to Control 5.28 as it establishes processes for managing major incidents, including documentation requirements, communication protocols, and root cause analysis. These procedures ensure that critical security events are properly documented and evidence is preserved throughout the incident lifecycle, including reporting hours, maintaining incident records, and conducting post-incident analysis.

## 6. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy is relevant to Control 5.28 as it addresses the organization's commitment to comply with legal and regulatory obligations across different jurisdictions. Since evidence collection must be admissible in courts and meet various jurisdictional requirements, this policy ensures that evidence management procedures consider applicable legal frameworks and contractual obligations.
