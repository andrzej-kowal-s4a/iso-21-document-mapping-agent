# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.14 Information Transfer

## 1. Encryption and Cryptography Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170105815

This policy directly addresses the control requirement for protecting information in transit through encryption. It establishes standards for encrypting data in transit via VPN connections, encrypted transmission protocols, and approved ciphers. It covers protection of information transferred outside secure systems and encryption requirements for both electronic transfers and portable storage devices.

## 2. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy provides the foundation for information transfer controls by defining information sensitivity levels (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable). It establishes labeling requirements and handling rules for different classification levels, which are essential for implementing appropriate transfer controls commensurate with information classification as required by the control.

## 3. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy governs acceptable use of IT resources including rules for email, instant messaging, internet use, and communication channels. It addresses prevention of unauthorized disclosure through proper use of electronic communication facilities, protection against phishing, and proper handling of data during electronic transfers.

## 4. Mobile Device Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106965

This policy addresses information transfer via mobile devices, covering security requirements for devices that store or transfer company data. It establishes controls for protecting data on portable devices, authentication requirements, and restrictions on storing sensitive data on mobile devices, supporting secure information transfer.

## 5. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106633

This policy extends information transfer controls to personal devices used for business purposes. It addresses secure remote access methods, VPN requirements, encryption of devices, and restrictions on storing classified information on BYOD devices, ensuring information transferred via personal devices remains protected.

## 6. Network General Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104105

This policy establishes controls for network-based information transfer including VPN requirements, firewall configurations, network segmentation, and secure remote connections. It addresses protection of information in transit through encrypted networks and authentication controls for network access.

## 7. Supplier Security Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099594

This policy addresses information transfer to and from third parties by establishing security requirements for suppliers who may access or receive company information. It includes confidentiality requirements, data security controls, and secure data handling provisions for external parties, supporting the control's requirements for transfer agreements with third parties.

## 8. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy ensures that information transfer practices comply with applicable legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements including GDPR and other privacy laws. It supports the control's requirement to consider legal and regulatory requirements related to information transfer, including requirements for electronic signatures and data protection.
