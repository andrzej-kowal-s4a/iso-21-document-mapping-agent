# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.5 Protecting against physical and environmental threats

## 1. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This procedure is directly relevant to control 7.5 as it addresses protection against environmental disasters and ensures business continuity in case of catastrophic events. The Disaster Recovery Plan establishes procedures to recover from physical and environmental threats such as natural disasters, fires, floods, or other incidents that could impact infrastructure availability.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy includes a dedicated Physical Security section that defines requirements for physical security controls, including security perimeters, access controls, protection of equipment from physical and environmental hazards, and visitor management. It establishes the foundation for protecting information processing facilities against physical and environmental threats.

## 3. Office Alarm Code
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107751

This procedure implements physical access controls for office premises, including alarm system management for securing facilities outside business hours. It directly supports control 7.5 by establishing procedures to protect physical premises from unauthorized access and ensuring proper security monitoring of facilities.

## 4. Alarm codes management
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520699

This internal helpdesk procedure establishes the management of alarm codes for physical access control systems. It supports control 7.5 by ensuring proper administration of physical security systems that protect facilities from unauthorized access and physical threats, including procedures for adding and removing user codes.

## 5. After-Hours workplace activity procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104529

This bilingual procedure addresses health, safety, and security requirements when staff work after hours or use office space outside normal hours. It supports control 7.5 by establishing rules for maintaining facility security and safety during non-standard hours, including requirements for alarm code access and responsibility for facility security.

## 6. Hardware insurance list procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/334365211

This procedure maintains an inventory of insured hardware assets and ensures proper protection through insurance coverage. It supports control 7.5 by providing financial protection against losses from physical and environmental threats, ensuring that critical equipment can be replaced if damaged by disasters, theft, or other physical threats.

## 7. Risk Management Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170100789

This policy establishes the framework for identifying, analyzing, and managing risks across the organization, including physical and environmental risks. It directly supports control 7.5's guidance requirement to perform risk assessments to identify potential consequences of physical and environmental threats and implement necessary safeguards.

## 8. Server management procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170107313

This procedure governs the lifecycle management of server infrastructure, including requirements for physical server location, resource allocation, and environment setup. It supports control 7.5 by ensuring proper management and protection of critical information processing infrastructure against physical and environmental risks.
