# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.4 Management responsibilities

## 1. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This is the umbrella information security policy that defines security responsibilities across the organization, including management responsibilities for ensuring personnel apply security in accordance with established policies. It explicitly addresses separation of duties, personnel security, access management, and states that "Smart4Aviation Group assigns security-related responsibilities to individuals and groups" with a detailed Security Responsibilities schedule.

## 2. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure directly addresses control requirement (a) by ensuring personnel are properly briefed on their security roles and responsibilities prior to being granted access. It includes security awareness training assignment via BambooHR, mandatory GDPR documentation review, and assignment of first tasks with clear expectations from management during the onboarding period.

## 3. Training Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106783

This policy supports control requirements (d) and (f) by ensuring personnel achieve appropriate levels of security awareness and continue professional development. It defines mandatory and optional training, funding mechanisms, and requires management approval of training requests, demonstrating management's active role in ensuring personnel maintain appropriate security skills and qualifications.

## 4. Training Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106790

This procedure provides detailed work instructions for processing training requests, supporting control requirement (f) for ongoing professional education. It requires manager approval and specification of time allocation for training, and addresses requirement (h) by ensuring personnel are provided with adequate resources and time for implementing security-related processes through structured training programs.

## 5. Policy Owner vs Author of procedures, standards, guidances and processes
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102632

This policy establishes clear management responsibilities for creating, implementing, and maintaining security policies and procedures. It defines Policy Owner responsibilities including establishing implementation plans, training programs, review cycles, and ensuring compliance - directly supporting requirement (c) that personnel are mandated to fulfill information security policies.

## 6. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure ensures management maintains control over information security during employee departures, addressing requirement (e) regarding compliance with terms of employment. It includes knowledge transfer responsibilities, asset recovery, access revocation, and exit conversations - all managed by personnel managers demonstrating their information security responsibilities.

## 7. WHISTLEBLOWER: Internal Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/1205665906

This procedure directly addresses control requirement (g) by providing personnel with a confidential channel for reporting violations of information security policy and procedures. It establishes whistleblower@smart4aviation.aero as the reporting mechanism with provisions for anonymous reporting and protection from retaliatory actions, ensuring only authorized personnel handle reports.

## 8. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure supports requirement (g) by establishing another channel for reporting security-related violations and incidents. It makes clear that reporting is not intended to blame or penalize anyone, encourages reporting of security incidents, and defines management responsibilities for ensuring all personnel understand their obligation to report security incidents immediately.
