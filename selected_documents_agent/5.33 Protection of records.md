# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.33 Protection of records

## 1. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy establishes the framework for classifying information assets based on sensitivity levels (Secret, Private, Restricted, Sensitive, Disclosable) and defines handling rules for each classification. It directly supports the control's requirement to protect records from unauthorized access and release by establishing clear guidelines on how different types of records should be stored, handled, and protected based on their classification.

## 2. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy addresses the proper disposal, sanitization, and destruction of media containing sensitive and classified information. It directly supports the control's requirement to prevent unauthorized release and ensure proper disposal of records after their retention period, protecting against falsification and unauthorized access during the disposal process.

## 3. Disposal of electronic equipment [#hardware #utilisation] - procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170108048

This procedure provides detailed steps for disposing of electronic hardware and data storage devices, including protocols for destruction to prevent data recovery. It directly implements the control's requirements for protecting records from loss and unauthorized access during disposal, ensuring chain of custody is maintained through documented utilization protocols and 5-year record retention.

## 4. Encryption and Cryptography Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170105815

This policy mandates encryption for data at rest and in transit, key management practices, and cryptographic standards. It directly supports the control's requirement to protect records from unauthorized access, falsification, and unauthorized release by ensuring confidentiality and integrity of records through cryptographic controls, and addresses the guidance on retaining cryptographic keys for encrypted archives.

## 5. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy establishes the framework for compliance with applicable laws and regulations including data retention requirements. It directly supports the control's purpose of ensuring compliance with legal, statutory, regulatory and contractual requirements related to records protection and availability, and helps determine appropriate retention periods for different types of records.

## 7. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This disaster recovery plan ensures the availability and recoverability of critical records and systems in the event of disasters or disruptions. It directly supports the control's requirement to protect records from loss and destruction, ensuring business continuity and that records remain accessible and usable throughout their retention period as required by the control guidance.

## 8. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security framework including information classification, access management, and IT asset management. It supports the control by providing the foundational security requirements for protecting records' confidentiality, integrity, and availability, and establishes responsibilities for record owners and handlers to ensure proper protection throughout the record lifecycle.
