# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 7.7 Clear desk and clear screen

## 1. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy directly addresses clear screen requirements by mandating that users must "lock screens when temporarily leaving the device that is in use" and "follow any other instructions regarding computer hygiene described in Rules of Personal Data Protection and Clean Desk Policy." It explicitly links to and references the Clean Desk Policy, establishing the framework for protecting information on desks and screens.

## 2. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overall security framework and includes requirements for physical security stating that "information processing and storage facilities shall have defined security perimeter, appropriate security barriers and access controls." It provides the foundational requirements that clear desk and clear screen policies implement, particularly regarding protecting information technology equipment from unauthorized access.

## 3. Mobile Device Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106965

This policy directly supports the clear desk and clear screen control by requiring that mobile devices "be configured in such way that it forces user to authenticate against it prior using" and "have auto-locking enabled." It mandates users take "all reasonable and appropriate precautions to keep control of the device and prevent unauthorized physical access," which aligns with the clear desk principle of securing devices when unattended.

## 4. Bring Your Own Device (BYOD) Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106633

This policy complements clear desk and clear screen requirements by mandating that BYOD devices must "set a unique personal identification number (PIN) or password" and have "automatically locking a device after an idle period." It requires users to "take all reasonable and appropriate precautions to keep control of the device and prevent unauthorized physical access," supporting the control's goal of protecting information when devices are unattended.

## 5. Procedura korzystnia z biura po godzinach pracy / After-Hours workplace activity procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104529

This procedure addresses clear desk requirements for after-hours work, requiring staff members to "take all reasonably practicable steps to ensure their own health, safety and facility security." It establishes protocols for securing the workplace when it is vacated, which directly supports the clear desk control's requirement to protect information "outside normal working hours."

## 6. General Information Media Disposal Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104943

This policy supports clear desk requirements by addressing the proper disposal of media including "hard drives, diskettes, tape cartridges, CDs, ribbons, hard copies, print-outs, and other similar items." It explicitly references the Clean Desk Policy and ensures that sensitive information on removable storage media and papers is properly secured and disposed of when no longer required.

**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure ensures clear desk compliance during employee departure by requiring the recovery of organization assets, revoking access privileges, and ensuring proper handover of information. It supports the control by preventing sensitive information from being left unattended during the transition period and ensuring all company assets including documents and devices are properly secured and returned.

## 8. Employee Offboarding
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4AIH/pages/149520666

This detailed technical procedure ensures physical security and clear desk compliance during offboarding by systematically removing access to systems, recovering hardware, and ensuring all sensitive information is secured. It includes specific steps to ensure that when employees leave, no sensitive information remains on desks, screens, or accessible locations, directly supporting the clear desk and clear screen control requirements.
