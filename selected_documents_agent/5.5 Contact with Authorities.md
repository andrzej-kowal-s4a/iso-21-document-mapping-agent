# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 5.5 Contact with Authorities

## 1. Security Incident Reporting Procedure / Procedura zgłaszania incydentów związanych z bezpieczeństwem
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure is directly relevant as it establishes the formal process for reporting security incidents, which is essential for determining when authorities should be contacted. It provides the framework for identifying incidents that may require reporting to law enforcement or regulatory authorities, supporting the control's requirement to specify when and by whom authorities should be contacted.

## 2. Personal Data Protection Breach Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170099350

This procedure is critical for contact with supervisory authorities (UODO/DPA) in case of personal data breaches. It explicitly defines when notification to the supervisory authority is required, timeline requirements (72 hours), notification formats, and contact methods, directly supporting the control's guidance on timely reporting of incidents to regulatory and supervisory authorities.

## 3. Legal and Regulatory Compliance Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106479

This policy establishes the organization's commitment to compliance with applicable laws and regulations, including maintaining awareness of regulatory expectations. It demonstrates the framework for understanding and monitoring legal obligations from various authorities in the Netherlands, Poland, and the EU, which aligns with the control's guidance on facilitating understanding of authorities' expectations.

## 4. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy defines security objectives, responsibilities, and incident management requirements that underpin contact with authorities. It establishes the security incident management framework and references customer data security requirements, which are essential context for determining when regulatory or law enforcement authorities need to be contacted regarding security matters.

## 5. S4A Disaster Recovery Plan
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101158

This plan is relevant to the control's reference to contingency planning and business continuity processes. It relates to the guidance's mention of contacts with emergency services, utilities, and other authorities that support business continuity and disaster recovery, ensuring appropriate coordination with external parties during major incidents.

## 6. Communication procedure in case of Major Incident
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170102638

This procedure defines the communication process during major incidents, which may require contact with authorities. It establishes clear escalation and notification channels that could be extended to include regulatory authorities when major incidents involve security breaches or service disruptions requiring external notification.
