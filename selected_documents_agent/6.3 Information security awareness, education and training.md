# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 6.3 Information security awareness, education and training

## 1. Training Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106783

This policy directly addresses the control requirements by establishing the organization's commitment to training and developing team members. It covers mandatory and optional training types, funding, training methods, and ensures alignment with strategic objectives. The policy specifically addresses key professional and skills-based learning requirements essential for information security awareness and education.

## 2. Training Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106790

This procedure provides detailed implementation guidance for the training policy, including how to request, approve, and deliver training programs. It specifies the process for tracking training completion through JIRA, evaluating training effectiveness through ratings and feedback, and ensures that team members receive appropriate education relevant to their job functions, which directly supports ISO 27001 control 6.3.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella security policy establishes the foundation for information security awareness requirements. Article on Personnel Security explicitly states that "upon starting work, Smart4Aviation Group employees shall receive general security awareness training" and that "the security awareness training for every employee is then refreshed annually," directly fulfilling the control's requirements for periodic awareness training.

## 4. Onboarding Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure ensures that new personnel receive initial information security awareness training as part of their onboarding process. It includes assignment of training materials via BambooHR, mandatory HR-related documents and GDPR documentation, and cybersecurity training ticket creation, ensuring personnel are aware of information security responsibilities from day one.

## 5. Security Incident Reporting Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104051

This procedure supports the awareness aspect of the control by providing clear guidance on how personnel should identify and report security incidents. It serves as an educational tool that helps personnel understand their information security responsibilities regarding incident awareness and reporting, which is a key component of security awareness programs.

## 6. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy educates personnel on appropriate use of information technology resources, covering passwords, data protection, communication, internet use, and device security. It provides specific guidance on information security responsibilities that personnel must fulfill in their daily activities, directly supporting the awareness and education objectives of control 6.3.

## 7. Personnel Vetting Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104805

This policy ensures that personnel handling information above disclosable sensitivity levels are properly vetted, which relates to the control's emphasis on ensuring personnel understand their information security responsibilities based on their job function and access level. It establishes baseline security awareness requirements based on the sensitivity of information personnel will access.

## 8. Offboarding Procedure
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure ensures continuity of security awareness throughout the employee lifecycle, including knowledge transfer requirements and ensuring departing personnel understand their ongoing security responsibilities. It complements the onboarding procedure by addressing security awareness requirements when personnel leave or change roles with different information security requirements.
