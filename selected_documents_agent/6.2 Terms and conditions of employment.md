# Selected Documents for ISO 27001 Control 6.2 - Terms and conditions of employment

## 1. Onboarding Procedure / Procedura wdrożenia
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104063

This procedure directly addresses the onboarding process where employment agreements are established and personnel are introduced to their information security responsibilities. It outlines how new employees receive security awareness training, sign mandatory HR-related documents, and are assigned roles that define their access rights, all of which are essential components of employment terms and conditions.

## 2. Personnel Vetting Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104805

This policy is directly relevant to the control as it defines pre-employment processes including identity verification, background checks, and vetting procedures that must be completed before personnel are granted access to information and assets. It ensures personnel suitability is assessed before employment terms are finalized, addressing the control's guidance on verifying candidates during pre-employment.

## 3. Information Security Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104722

This umbrella policy establishes the overarching framework for information security responsibilities across the organization. It defines security responsibilities, roles, and obligations that must be communicated to personnel through employment agreements. The policy directly supports the requirement for employment contracts to state personnel responsibilities for information security.

## 4. Offboarding procedure / Procedura odejścia z firmy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170104057

This procedure is essential for addressing the control's guidance on responsibilities that continue after employment ends. It ensures proper handling of information security obligations during termination, including return of assets, revocation of access rights, and knowledge transfer, supporting the requirement for post-employment responsibilities defined in employment terms.

## 5. Information Technology Acceptable Use Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106001

This policy defines specific rules and responsibilities for using the organization's IT resources, which should be referenced in employment agreements. It clarifies personnel obligations regarding data protection, password management, communication, and device usage, directly supporting the control's requirement to state information security responsibilities in contractual agreements.

## 6. Information Classification Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101881

This policy is relevant to the control's guidance point (c) regarding responsibilities for classification and management of information. It defines how personnel should handle information based on sensitivity levels, which must be communicated through employment terms and conditions to ensure personnel understand their obligations for protecting organizational assets.

## 7. Training Policy
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170106783

This policy supports the control by establishing mandatory security awareness training requirements that ensure personnel understand their information security responsibilities. It defines how the organization communicates security roles and obligations to personnel, which complements the employment contractual agreements and helps personnel fulfill their security duties.

## 8. Personal Data Protection Policy (Polityka Bezpieczeństwa Danych Osobowych)
**Confluence URL:** https://smart4aviation.atlassian.net/wiki/spaces/S4A/pages/170101280

This policy is critical for defining personnel responsibilities regarding personal data processing, which is referenced in the control's guidance point (b) on legal responsibilities including data protection legislation. It establishes authorizations, obligations, and security requirements that must be included in employment agreements to ensure GDPR compliance and proper data handling.
